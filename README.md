# What is this Repository??
The purpose of this repository is to catalogue my journey into teaching myself Quantum Computing and High Performance Computing. I officially started this journey in late June of 2026 and will follow a roadmap of prerequisites and projects along the way to show my ability level at each step. I will attempt to regularly update a learning log of what topics I learned, as well as explain a bit in my own words, for reasons of seeing and showing my progress and keeping accountability to myself. I hope that people will see the improvement I have in coding and starting on GitHub from the time of writing this to hopefully a professional level at the very end. This roadmap starts with the very basic prerequisites, which I will review some of if I feel not completely comfortable with them or if it has been a long time since encountering them. It will then go on to learning all of the math, coding, and physics topics needed to be an intership/research ready candidate and eventually an industry-ready candidate in Quantum Computing with HPC. This journey is expected to be at least 2+ years but may vary due to time and commitment as a busy upcoming junior Physics undergrad.
